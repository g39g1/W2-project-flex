About Simple Resume


Simple Resume is a brand of the nationally renowned career services
company Employment BOOST. As one of the top-rated resume writing
companies in the world, Employment BOOST has worked with a wide
range of clients ranging from Fortune 500 Executives to NFL players
in career transition.


Employment BOOST has traveled across the country providing workshops
and seminars to companies, government agencies, and academic
institutions including Purdue University, Kettering University,
Fordham University, Lowe’s, and the Food & Drug Administration
(FDA), to name a few.


As a brand, Simple Resume utilizes the same professional resume
writing best practices that Employment BOOST has built a 5-star
reputation on.


After all, your resume is a representation of your best achievements
and accomplishments. It’s important to get your resume right!



Why Simple Resume?
